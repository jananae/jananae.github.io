About

Hi! I’m Janae, a UX/UI designer passionate about making the world easier to navigate through better design.

I graduated from UCSD in 2021 with a Bachelor's in Communication and CogSci Design. My coursework provided me not only with an understanding of design principles, but also a strong desire to take on the social responsibilities that come with being a designer.

In the past I’ve worked on UX projects for a small side business focusing on handcrafted goods, as well as a hair care app designed for a speculative future. I've also been in charge of the media subdivision of a high school robotics team, coordinating our artists and animators to maintain the team’s visual brand.

In my free time, I enjoy doing archery, making small indie games, and falling in love with the neighborhood cats.
