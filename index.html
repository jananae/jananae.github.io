About Me

Hi there! I’m a UI/UX designer and digital content creator based in California. I graduated from UCSD in 2021 with a B.A. in Communication and B.S. in Cognitive Science Design. My education provided me not only with a strong understanding of design principles, but also a desire to take on the social responsibilities that come with being a designer.

In the past I’ve worked on UX projects for a small side business making handcrafted goods, as well as a hair care app designed for a speculative future. I've also done work in brand design, graphic design, and 3D animation for various non-profits and student organizations.

In my spare time I enjoy sewing, making small indie games in Bitsy (on itch.io!), and curating memes for my friends.
