GEn1E Site Redesign

Home page layout changes and visual asset creation for a biotech company

Overview

As a design intern at GEn1E Lifesciences, I was tasked with solving a few main concerns with the company site's homepage. The first of these was the site's lack of visual appeal: while the overall aesthetic was clean and followed company branding, it leaned too minimalist and wasn't as engaging as they would've liked. Second, some of the text was wordy and could be hard to follow for someone not versed in biotech jargon, and needed to be rewritten. I was also given free reign to make suggestions for other areas of improvement that I identified, of which there were a few.


I provided two main mockups with mix-and-match elements, with the above mockup being the heavily preferred style.
