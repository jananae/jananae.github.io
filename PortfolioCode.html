About

Hi! I’m Janae, a Communication and Design student at UC San Diego. I’m passionate about making the world easier to navigate through better design. My main focus is on UX/UI, but I’ll dabble in a little bit of every type of design when I can.

In the past I’ve been in charge of the media subdivision of my high school robotics team, coordinating our artists and animators to maintain the team’s visual brand. Nowadays, I’m expanding on my skills in college through design-centered coursework and supplementing that through my communication courses with a more theoretical understanding of the social responsibility that comes with design.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy doing archery, decorating homemade desserts, and falling in love with the neighborhood cats.
